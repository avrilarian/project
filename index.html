EDITOR's NOTE

Minimal is more.

April, 2016

A teacher of mine once told me that change is the only constant in life. That’s certainly true for the fashion industry, which has spent the better part of the century working tirelessly to change Fashion for the better. To be precise, it is one of the constantly changing and ever-evolving industry in the world. This very aspect combined with the fact that fashion and media have become so intertwined and inseparable is what made us come out with a journal on fashion.
Fashion is slightly elusive and extremely seductive with the power to transform an image and make a social statement.


In India, though the fashion industry has grown tremendously, it is still at its infancy at the moment when compared to the global arena yet it has great potential to make the mark on the world stage.


In this issue, we analyse the fashion industry in India, how it differs from the world, the way the industry works, the scope of having a career, the role social media plays in fashion, how there has been a growing consciousness among Indian men and women towards the fashion style and senses. We also bring
to light the lesser known side of fashion and its contribution to the social development.


Thus we unveil the journal, “INVOGUE” with its fresh look that is elegant, eye-catching and easy to navigate.


We hope you like what you see.
